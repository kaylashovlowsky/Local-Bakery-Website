Local Bakery & Cafe

Welcome to the Local Bakery & Cafe

This charming bakery is nestled below the Cascade Mountains in Local, Washington. We offer delicious bakery staples and a variety of originally designed and made-to-order treats. Stop in for a donut, pastry, fresh loaf of bread, or try one of our seasonal favorites.

Our bakery has been a cornerstone of the Local's downtown for generations, and we are proud to continue the traditions.
